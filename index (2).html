What would be like if suddenly every human disappeared?

Payback question for science:

Humans have survived on the Earth for 2.5 million years and we have definitely made a dent on this Earth. We have caused toxic chemicals to get into the environment and have almost gone extinct a couple of times from the Black death to the influenza pandemic. But, what would really happen if we all suddenly disappeared from the planet? The first thing that would happen is that nature would take over our house and especially the ones with wood. Termites would eat through the wood and destroy it. In cold areas, pipes would freeze and eventually burst. Animals would all have to fend for themselves, including cats and dogs, and a lot would go extinct due to the larger animals on the planet eating them. Cities would start to be overgrown and turn back into forest. The human legacy would probably disappear after 15,000-20,000 years. But, who is to say the future scientist wouldn't be able to test certain things. Humans disappearing suddenly or going extinct is very very unlikely but who knows what could really happen?
